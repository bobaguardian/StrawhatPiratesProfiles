Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro, also known as Pirate Hunter Zoro, is the combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates and a former bounty hunter. He was the first member to join the crew, and is considered one of the most dangerous members in it after Luffy. His dream is to become the greatest swordsman in the world.
